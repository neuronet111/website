Psychological Workshop

Digital Minds, Disturbed Souls ‚Äî The Wheel of Emotions

An interactive workshop rooted in psychoanalytic understanding to help students explore emotions, motivation, and behavioral patterns.

About the Workshop

Through the Wheel of Emotions, students learn to identify feelings, understand triggers, and develop healthier relationships with technology and themselves.

Core Psychology Modules

Emotional Awareness & Regulation

Understanding emotions, identifying triggers, regulation tools, and emotional vocabulary.

Addiction, Habit Cycle & Dopamine

Dopamine loops, screen addiction, digital habits, reward patterns, and how to break or build cycles.

Rewards, Triggers & Habits

How habits form, why we repeat behaviors, what reinforces actions, and how to change patterns.

Defence Mechanisms

How the mind unconsciously protects itself; recognizing projection, denial, displacement, rationalisation, etc.

Cognitive Biases

How we perceive the world inaccurately; social media influence, confirmation bias, attention bias, etc.

Social Psychology

Peer pressure, conformity, group behavior, social comparison, digital identity, and belonging.

Communication & Emotional Intelligence

Active listening, empathy, boundary-setting, interpersonal communication, expressing emotions safely.
